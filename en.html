09 · FAQ

Questions we always get.

Don't see yours? Drop us a line at hola@idealica.com and we'll reply in under 24h.

How much does a website with you cost?
Depends on scope, but we give you fixed price before kickoff. Corporate sites start at €1,500 and a typical e-commerce is around €4,000–8,000. You get a detailed proposal within 48h of the first call.
How long until I have it live?
Most projects ship in 1 to 3 weeks. We work in short sprints with a weekly demo: you see progress, propose changes, and we apply them mid-sprint — not at the end.
I don't know what kind of AI I need. Can you help?
Yes, that's exactly our first call. You tell us the problem (not the solution you think you need), and we tell you whether AI is the path or whether an automation solves it for a tenth of the cost. We say "no" a lot.
Who's actually on the team? 1 person, 5, 50?
We're small on purpose. Core of 3 (design, product, engineering) plus specific collaborators when a project needs them. When you hire Idealica, you talk to the people who build. Full stop.
What happens after handoff?
30 days of support included: training, tweaks, questions. After that you can sign up for monthly maintenance (from €350/mo) or keep the code and run it yourself. No lock-in.
Do you work with startups or only established SMBs?
Both. What matters is that you know what you want to build. If you come saying "I want a pretty site because we should", we're probably not a fit. If you come with a concrete problem and a real customer on the other side, yes.
What if we hire you and it doesn't work out?
That's why we do the 30-min call up front. If there's no chemistry or the project doesn't suit us, we tell you on that call and point you to someone who fits better. We never sign something we don't believe will go well.
